# Submit Your Website to Google Search Console - CRITICAL STEP

## Why You See "No information available for this page"

Google hasn't indexed your site yet. This is NORMAL for new websites. To fix this:

## Step 1: Go to Google Search Console
Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console

## Step 2: Add Your Property
1. Click "+ Add Property"
2. Choose "URL prefix"
3. Enter: https://origiganicsbywallian.com
4. Click "Continue"

## Step 3: Verify Ownership
Google will show verification methods. Choose "HTML tag":

Your verification code is ALREADY in your website:
```html
<meta name="google-site-verification" content="dng7zcSpKaPWp29xMc_m3jpNzergMq6XKN5QH9DovsM" />
```

1. Click "Verify" button
2. Google will confirm ✅

## Step 4: Submit Your Sitemap
1. In Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"

Your sitemap is already live at:
https://origiganicsbywallian.com/sitemap.xml

## Step 5: Request Indexing
1. In Search Console, click "URL Inspection" (top)
2. Enter: https://origiganicsbywallian.com
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Wait 1-2 minutes
5. Click "Request Indexing" button

## Step 6: Submit Each Important Page
Repeat URL Inspection for:
- https://origiganicsbywallian.com/products
- https://origiganicsbywallian.com/about
- https://origiganicsbywallian.com/blog
- https://origiganicsbywallian.com/contact
